Who am I?

Video game passionate, always looking to find new ways of playing.
As a huge defender of weird feelings, my purpose is to help others learn through
a playfully gameplay. At the end, I think that the best type of fun is to learn new things.

My journey

As most people, I grew up sorrounded of games and playfully enviroments. Video games entered early in my life, with the classics GameBoy and PlayStation 2. More console generations came, and my interest in video games grew with them. My big brother made me understand the fun of playing alongside other people and, when I was older, suggested me to read Mark Fisher, who introduced me into the good weirdness of the world, encouraging me to think over the things that we don't know and embrace this feeling.

When I had the chance, still as a child, I took a video game course, which introduced me to the game development. Years later, I coursed a Video Game Design and Development degree in the CITM, Terrassa (Barcelona).

When I finished my degree I entered in Univrse, a VR company, working as a QA. I'm always looking to evolve and ambrace new challenges. This hasn't stop me to create my own video games in my free time with Ginuh Games, a company I founded alongside Erik Martín and Juande García, two friends who wanted, as me, create immortal memories for the people.

Now, we are working on a cooperative game. We think that a game is better playing it with friends.
